# Mantle Relevance Layer

## Purpose

The project should not fake Mantle-native coverage. It should classify relevance explicitly.

## Categories

- `mantle_native` — deployed on Mantle or directly part of Mantle ecosystem
- `rwa_distribution` — relevant to on-chain RWA distribution
- `tokenized_assets` — tokenized stocks, funds or securities-like assets
- `prediction_markets` — AI or market-based forecasting systems
- `ai_agent_finance` — AI agents in finance workflows
- `defi_liquidity` — liquidity, lending, DEX, yield or routing primitive
- `ecosystem_scouting` — useful for ecosystem or grant-funnel review
- `not_relevant` — no strong connection to Mantle thesis

## Rule

If relevance is weak, say it is weak. Do not relabel random degen signals as Mantle-native.
