# Product Brief — Mantle Signal-to-Thesis Console

## One-liner

A self-hosted AI research console that turns fragmented Web3 ecosystem signals into structured thesis, evidence, risk flags, and human-confirmed decision trails.

## Product

We are building a product layer for an existing read-only AI research contour.

The scanner can find signals. The console makes them usable:

- normalizes the signal
- stores evidence
- computes risk flags
- classifies Mantle relevance
- generates a research note
- requires a human decision
- preserves the trail

## User

Primary users:

- ecosystem scouts
- grant / accelerator reviewers
- Web3 researchers
- small research teams
- degen operators who need discipline before acting

## Pain

Raw signals are noisy. Most dashboards only display metrics. Most AI summaries are not reproducible. The user needs an auditable object that answers:

- why did this surface?
- what evidence supports it?
- what is missing?
- what are the risks?
- is it relevant to Mantle?
- what should a human check next?

## Why now

AI agents are moving from chat UX to operational workflows. Web3 ecosystems need scouting and research systems that are transparent, reproducible, and safe enough to use without private keys or execution.

## Core thesis

Every early signal should become a traceable research object.
