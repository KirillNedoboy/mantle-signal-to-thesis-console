# Demo Script

## 90-second version

### 0–10s — framing

"This is Mantle Signal-to-Thesis Console. It is not a trading bot. It is a self-hosted research console that turns autonomous scanner outputs into structured research objects."

### 10–25s — inbox

Open the Signal Inbox.

Show that signals have:

- source
- chain
- category
- status
- risk count
- Mantle relevance
- age

### 25–55s — detail page

Open one signal.

Show:

- evidence panel
- risk flags
- score
- Mantle relevance
- generated research note
- next manual checks

Say:

"The agent does not say buy. It says why the signal surfaced, what is missing, what risk exists, and what a human should verify next."

### 55–75s — decision trail

Show decision trail.

Say:

"Every decision is durable. The signal becomes an auditable research object, not a disappearing Telegram alert."

### 75–90s — Mantle angle

Say:

"For Mantle, this can serve ecosystem scouting: RWA, tokenized assets, DeFi liquidity, AI-native finance and grant-funnel review. The workflow is repeatable and open-source."
